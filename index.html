About Rachel

With over a decade of experience capturing life's most precious moments, I specialize in creating timeless, authentic photographs that tell your family's unique story. Based in Orange County, I love incorporating the beautiful cityscape and natural settings into sessions that feel relaxed and genuine.

My approach is gentle and patient, especially with weddings and young families. I believe the best photos happen when families feel comfortable and natural, allowing their true personalities to shine through.
